# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participate in a way that is respectful, technically honest, and focused on improving the project.

Examples of expected behavior:

- give direct, constructive technical feedback
- assume good intent and discuss disagreements on the merits
- share context when reporting bugs, security concerns, or design objections

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language
- bad-faith arguments, personal attacks, or intimidation
- sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or its contributors.
